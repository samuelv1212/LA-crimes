    Visualizing LA Crime Data

    Our goal is to provide insights into the type of crimes that occur in LA, the locations of those crimes, and the demographics of the victims of those crimes. We hope these insights into crime can inform citizens of LA and law enforcement to keep LA knowledgeable and safe. Please visit the links in the navigation bar to view our data.
